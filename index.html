Zhaoyuan Xu

I am currently pursuing my M.S. degree with the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) and my supervisor is Prof. Wei Gong. I received my B.E. degree from USTC, in 2020. I am focusing on developing Internet of Things (IoT) systems with backscatter technology to enable the vision of pervasive communication. I am also interested in wireless networks.

Recent News:

  • Our recent work Poster: Enhanced ZigBee Backscatter Communication using Fine-Grained Chip-Level Modulation is accepted at ACM MobiSys 2023.
  • Our recent work BumbleBee: Enabling the Vision of Pervasive ZigBee Backscatter Communication is accepted at IEEE PerCom 2023.
  • Our recent work Enabling ZigBee Backscatter Communication in a Crowded Spectrum is accepted at IEEE ICNP 2022.
